Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a meat and pasta bsed dish originating in Italy

The dish involves stacking layers of pasta and meat on top of each other that is why some people refer to it as a pasta cake

Ingredients
  • 1 package Lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • halfa pound of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • half a pound of cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
Steps
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  4. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain.
  5. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well.
  7. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
